Privacy & Terms

What This Generator Does

This page lets you create a downloadable QR vCard directly in your browser. Your contact details are turned into a QR code and a vCard file without being stored on our servers.

New to QRvCard.io? Visit the How To page for step-by-step guidance, learn more About the project, or read our Privacy & Terms.

Your Info

Enter the contact details you want people to save when they scan your QR code. Only your Full Name and Email are required; the rest is optional.

Additional Info

Add any extra details you’d like to include in your vCard, such as your job title, organisation, website, or social profiles. These fields are optional.

QR Customization

Personalise the look of your QR code with your own colours and optional logo. This helps your QR vCard feel more on-brand when used on business cards, websites, or printed materials.

QR Code Label (Optional)

You can add a short label beneath your QR code, such as your name, company, or purpose. This is useful when printing or sharing the QR code in different contexts.

Generate

When you’re ready, generate your QR vCard and download it as a ZIP file. Your QR image and contact file are prepared locally in your browser.

What You’ll Download

Your download includes a QR code image and a vCard file, bundled together in a ZIP. This makes it easy to save, print, share, or upload your QR vCard wherever you need it.

  • QRCode.png — your generated QR code image
  • Contact.vcf — your contact details in standard vCard format

Best Ways to Use Your QR vCard

A QR vCard can be used on business cards, resumes, email signatures, websites, event badges, and printed materials — anywhere you want people to save your contact details quickly.
