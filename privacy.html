Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Your privacy matters. QR vCard is designed to run entirely in your browser. That means:

  • No personal data is collected, stored, or transmitted.
  • No accounts are required to use the site.
  • No server-side storage — your vCard data and QR code are created locally in your browser.
  • No tracking — we do not use cookies or analytics to monitor behavior.
