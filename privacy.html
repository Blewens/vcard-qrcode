Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important. Here's what we do — and what we don't do — with your data.

1. No Data Collection

QRvCard does not store, process, or transmit any personal information entered into the vCard form. All QR codes and vCard files are generated directly in your browser and remain on your device.

2. No User Accounts or Databases

We do not have user accounts, servers, or any backend storage. Your information is never saved or sent to us in any way.

3. Google AdSense and Third-Party Cookies

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertising. Google may use cookies or web beacons to serve ads based on your previous visits to this and other websites.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your interests. You can learn more and manage your ad settings by visiting:

  • Google Ads Settings
  • Google Advertising Policies

4. No Tracking or Analytics

We do not use Google Analytics or any other tracking technologies. We respect your privacy and believe your activity on this site should stay with you.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or your data, feel free to get in touch via the GitHub repository or contact link (if applicable).
