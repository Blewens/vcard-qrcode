What Is a QR vCard?

A vCard is the standard file format used to share contact details digitally — it's what your phone typically receives when someone texts you their info or AirDrops a contact. It contains all your key details like name, number, email, job title, and more.

A QR code is a scannable image that links directly to content — in this case, a downloadable vCard.

When combined, a QR vCard allows you to share your contact information instantly — no need to type anything or already have someone’s phone number. Just scan and save.

Perfect for business cards, resumes, email signatures, websites, or event networking.
