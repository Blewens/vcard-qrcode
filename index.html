What Is a QR vCard?

A vCard is a digital version of a business card — a standardized file format that stores contact information such as your name, phone number, email, job title, and more. It’s widely supported by phones, email apps, and contact managers.

A QR code is a type of barcode that can be scanned using any smartphone camera to instantly access information.

When combined, a QR vCard makes it effortless to share your contact info — scan the QR code, and your details are ready to save. No typing, no errors, no fuss.

It’s perfect for business cards, resumes, email signatures, networking events, websites, and more.
