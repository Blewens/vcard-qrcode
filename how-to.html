How to Use QR vCard

Follow these simple steps to create and customize your digital business card QR code.

Home Privacy Generate

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Enter your Full Name and Email (required).
  2. Optionally add details like organization, phone, address, job title, and social media links.
  3. Use the QR Customization section to change the foreground and background colors, or upload a logo.
  4. Click the Generate QR vCard button.
  5. Your QR code will appear below. You can then download it as an image and your contact as a .vcf file.

Label Your QR Code (Optional)

You can add a short text label below your QR code — for example, your name or company name.

  1. Enter your desired label text in the “Text Label” field.
  2. Select a font from the dropdown menu. We support several popular Google Fonts.
  3. The label will appear directly below your QR code and be included in the downloaded image.

Suggested Uses

  • Business Cards: Print your QR vCard on physical cards for instant contact saving.
  • Email Signatures: Include the QR in your signature for easy info sharing.
  • Phone Widgets: Save it to your home screen for quick sharing at events.
  • Event Badges: Ideal for networking — let others scan to save your details.
  • Portfolios or Resumes: Add it to printed or PDF versions for a modern touch.
  • Websites: Place your QR on your personal or business site under contact info.
